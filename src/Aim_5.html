AIM

 

  • To understand need for short term processing of speech.


  • To compute short term energy and study its significance.


  • To compute short term zero crossing rate and study its significance.


  • To compute short term autocorrelation and study its significance.


  • To estimate pitch of speech using short term autocorrelation.


  • To perform voiced/unvoiced/silence classification of speech using short term time domain parameters.
